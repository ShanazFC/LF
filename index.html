About La Fromagerie

Located in Old Town Alexandria, La Fromagerie is the perfect place to enjoy lovingly prepared dishes, cheese and meat boards, natural wine and craft beer in a relaxed atmosphere. We offer an always changing wine list of over 40 wines available by the glass, bottle or flight.

Chef/Owner Sebastien Tavel bases his cuisine fare around fresh seasonal food available from local farmers, particularly the heirloom and forageable produce that help support the biodiversity of our area.

Our Specialties

Locally Sourced Food

La Fromagerie brings a "farm to table" approach by purchasing directly from farmers or trusted suppliers. We believe in sustainable and responsible methods of farming, and are proud to support American artisan cheese making.

Expert Sommelier

With Sommelier Ben Kuna at the helm, La Fromagerie's extensive wine program focuses on natural, sustainable methods of production with an emphasis on organic and biodynamic pratices when possible.

WINE BAR & RETAIL SHOP

La Fromagerie features 40 wines by the glass and over 250 labels in the retail section.​ Sommelier Ben Kuna likes to keep guests entertained with weekly & monthly wine programs as well as seasonal wine dinners.
